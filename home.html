The Oklahoma Curling Club

We are the Oklahoma Curling Club, a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation formed in 2010 to promote the Olympic sport of curling in the state of Oklahoma. We offer open houses and Learn to Curl classes to the general public who would like to try curling. We also run curling leagues on Sunday evenings for those who would like to compete on a regular basis.
