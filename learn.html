Learn to Curl
If you are interested in curling and want to see what it's all about, our Learn to Curl events are perfect for you!
When See our Facebook page for dates
Cost $25/person
Time 2 Hours
Where Artic Edge Ice Arena
Our Learn to Curl (LTC) classes are a 2 hour class where we teach you the basics of curling (delivery, sweeping, scoring) and then play 2-3 full ends so you can see how it feels to play a full game. We have all the equipment required to participate. We ask that you dress warmish in layers and wear rubber soled tennis shoes. Our minimum age to participate is 15 years old and we require a parent or legal guardian be present to fill out the youth waiver and stay during the entire LTC for all minors.
