About the Oklahoma Curling Club

The Oklahoma Curling Club was founded in 2010 to promote the Olympic sport of curling in the state of Oklahoma. We play on Sunday evenings at the Arctic Edge Ice Arena in Edmond. We also offer curling classes and learn to curl sessions. If you would like to try curling come by one of our league nights. Our members will talk to you about the rules of the game and will explain how to join the club. You may also contact us by email at okcurling@gmail.com

Oklahoma Curling Club Rules of Play

Club specific rules can be found here. Please be sure to familiarize yourself with them before the start of each league. While rule changes are infrequent they do happen from time to time.

The US Curling Association also offers Rules of Curling for a more general guide.

Club members are expected to comply with the USCA's broom moratorium for all club events.
