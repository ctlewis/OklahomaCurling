Oklahoma Curling's Google Group

Be sure to check out the Oklahoma Curling Club's Google Group. It is a forum for members to communicate with each other. If you are interested in becoming a substitue for someone who can't make it to a league night this is the place to check first. You also just might find some good deals on equipment.

To sign up, go to the Group's Main Page and request membership. Once you are approved (usually one day) you can then create a profile.
