How to order merchandise

Print out this order form, mark which items/colors/sizes you want, and mail the order form with a check (the address is on the form). All pricing information is on the order form. Online ordering is not currently available.

If you want to see more detail, click on any of the item photos to open a larger version of the photo in another browser window or tab.

"On The Rocks" Fund­raiser

Help pay for our new carts and rock cooler by purchasing a message that will be engraved on the handles of one of our stones. Each handle engraving costs $300. Leave a lasting mark on the club.
