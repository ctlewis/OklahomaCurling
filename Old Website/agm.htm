Annual General Meeting

Hey everyone!

The 2016 OKCC Annual General Meeting will be held at  4pm, Sunday June 5th, at Arctic Edge.  To celebrate the end of another curling year, thank outgoing Officers/Directors and elect incoming Officer/Directors, we will also have a cookout!  The Club will provide the meat, but the rest is potluck so bring your favorite Summer dish to share with others!

Spouses are welcome.  I will need a head count to ensure we order enough meat, so please email me and let me know if you will be able to attend and if so how many by Monday 5/30.   The Club will also provide water and possibly other spirits!

Your attendance is VERY important as this is the meeting where YOU vote for YOUR Club Officers/Directors.

Heather Claxton
Special Programs
renvally19@yahoo.com

At the last BOD meeting according to our bylaws, the board of directors  elected and appointed replacements for resignations.

Kevin Hull, president-elect resigned and Jeremy Witzke will finish his term and become president next fiscal year.

Angela Strange, special programs resigned and was replaced by Heather for the remaining one year term.

Ryan McGee, communications resigned and was replaced by Andrew Stephens for the remaining one year term.

At the annual general meeting (June 5th), the membership will be asked to elect positions that are reaching the regular end of their terms.
