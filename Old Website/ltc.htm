Learn to Curl

Our Learn to Curl (LTC) classes are a 2 hour class where we teach you the basics of curling (delivery, sweeping, scoring) and then have you play 1.5-2 full ends so you can see how it feels to play a full game. Public LTC's are $25 per person and we have all the equipment required to participate. We ask that you dress warmish in layers and wear rubber soled tennis shoes (anything other than rubber soled tennis shoes will not be allowed on the ice). Our minimum age to participate is 15 years old and we require a parent or legal guardian be present to fill out the youth waiver and stay during the entire LTC for all minors. Our public LTC's will be posted here once we have them scheduled and on our Facebook page, Oklahoma Curling Club. Follow us on Facebook to see when we're having our events. (We have Learn to Curl classes before each of our leagues- Fall, Winter and Spring. We post our Learn to Curl events 3 weeks prior to each event.)

For a private LTC, we require a minimum non-refundable prepayment of 15 people at the $25 per person price. (If you have a group of less than 15, we would still require this amount -$375.00- to have a private LTC). This pays for the ice time we have to rent from the rink. The payment is non-refundable since if a group cancels at the last minute, we still have to pay the rink for the ice time. Your prepayment will need to be paid before we schedule the ice time with the rink. If you have over 15 people, then you can pay for the number of people over the 15 minimum closer to the date of your Learn to Curl. Each Learn to Curl can allow up to 60 people on the ice for the class. We allow beverages (including alcoholic) on the ice as long as they are in a plastic bottle or can (no glass is allowed on the ice).

We ask that you dress warmish in layers and wear rubber soled tennis shoes (anything other than rubber soled tennis shoes will not be allowed on the ice). Our minimum age to participate is 15 years old and we require a parent or legal guardian be present to fill out the youth waiver and stay during the entire LTC for all minors. We have all the equipment required to participate in a private LTC.

If you would like to book a private Learn to Curl, please email us at okcurling@gmail.com and we will work together to find a date and time that works for your group.
