Welcome!

We are the Oklahoma Curling Club, a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation formed in 2010 to promote the Olympic sport of curling in the state of Oklahoma. We offer open houses and Learn to Curl classes to the general public who would like to try curling. We also run curling leagues on Sunday evenings for those who would like to compete on a regular basis. Members of our club have participated in playdowns for national curling championships and in bonspiels (curling tournaments) all over North America.

If you have any questions about curling or our club, please send an email to okcurling@gmail.com and one of our board members will get in touch with you. Or to be kept informed of upcoming club events, add your email address to our mailing list.
