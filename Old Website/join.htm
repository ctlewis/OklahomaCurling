Join a league

Registration for the Fall League has now closed. Matches begin September 29th and go through November 10th.

If you need to, you can still pay both the league fee and annual membership fee online.

Annual membership includes a membership in the United States Curling Association (USA Curling) and the opportunity to sign up for leagues before the general public.

Alternates

If you are still interested in being involved then you may become an alternate for someone that is unable to complete the league schedule. If you are interested in becoming an alternate please send an email to okcurling@gmail.com.

Substitutes & Invited Guests

If you are a current league member interested in becoming a substitute or looking for someone to substitute for you then please visit the OK Curling Google Group. If you are not currently signed up then you can visit the group's main page and request membership. Non-members may become substitues as an "Invited Guest" but you will be required to fill out a liability waiver before you step on the ice. You are also responsible for providing your own equipment (broom and slider). The club does have a limited supply of loaner equipment.
