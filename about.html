The Oklahoma Curling Club
The Oklahoma Curling Club was founded in 2010 to promote the Olympic sport of curling in the state of Oklahoma. We play on Sunday evenings at the Artic Edge Ice Arena in Edmond. We also offer curling classes and learn to curl sessions. If you would like to try curling come by one of our league nights. Our members will talk to you about the rules of the game and will explain how to join the club. You may also contact us by email at okcurling@gmail.com
